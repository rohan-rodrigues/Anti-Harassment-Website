Safety First

Harassment is a big problem and needs to be stopped

Internationally, studies show that between 70-99% of women (stat from Stop Street Harassment) experience street harassment at some point during their lives.

Forty-eight percent of surveyed middle and high school students said they were sexually harassed at least once, typically by their peers, during the 2010-2011 school year, according to a report released Monday by the American Association of University Women (AAUW), a group that advocates for gender equality in schools.

About Us

Reidius is a social computer application that aids individuals who feel unsafe, or uncomfortable while being outside. The application includes multiple features that help in situations of harassment or bullying.


When someone feels unsafe outside, they can press a button and notify the police.
